Bachelor of Science in Psychology Research Papers
Cognitive restructuring and therapy for anxiety disorders
Beck, A.T., & Clark, D.A.
1996

This paper reviews cognitive therapy methods and their efficacy in treatment of anxiety disorders, focusing on restructuring maladaptive thought patterns.

Attachment theory and adult romantic relationships
Hazan, C., & Shaver, P.R.
1987

An exploration of how early attachment experiences influence patterns of adult romantic relationships and intimacy behaviors.

The social brain hypothesis
Dunbar, R.I.M.
2000

Proposes that the evolution of the human brain was driven by the need to manage complex social groups and relationships.

Mirror neurons and empathy
Iacoboni, M., et al.
1999

Investigates the role of mirror neurons in understanding others' actions and empathy development.

The role of executive functions in psychological disorders
Snyder, H.R.
2013

Discusses how deficits in executive functions contribute to the development and maintenance of common psychological disorders.
